What?

A "platform" is a system that can be programmed and therefore customized by outside users and in that way, adapted to countless needs and niches that the platform's original developers could not have possibly contemplated, much less had time to accommodate.

- Marc Anderseen

If you can build on top of it, it's a platform. If you can't, it's not.
